"Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah" (TMKOC) is a popular Indian television sitcom that has been airing since July 28, 2008. It is based on the weekly column "Duniya Ne Undha Chashma" written by columnist and journalist Taarak Mehta for the Gujarati magazine Chitralekha. The show is set in the fictional Gokuldham Co-operative Housing Society in Mumbai and revolves around the lives of its residents, addressing various social issues in a light-hearted and humorous manner. The characters of the show have become iconic and the sitcom has garnered a large and loyal fanbase over the years.
